What this site is about

This page is specifically about USDM / USD Montelibero. The ticker USDM is used by multiple projects; here the identity is the Stellar network plus the issuer address.

$

A dollar unit inside Montelibero

USDM is used as a dollar-denominated settlement token in the Montelibero ecosystem and on Stellar DEX. It is not a bank account or a dollar balance in your name.

1:1

Issuer obligation

The formula from Wiki and stellar.toml: upon holder request, the issuer undertakes to exchange USDM one-for-one for USDC on Stellar within 24 hours.

ID

Issuer matters most

A fake asset can use the same code USDM. The real USD Montelibero is: code USDM, issuer GDHDC4GBNPMENZAOBB4NCQ25TGZPDRK6ZGWUGSI22TVFATOLRPSUUSDM, domain mtl.montelibero.org.

≠

USDM is not USDC

USDC is issued by another issuer. USDM is a separate Stellar asset of the MTL Fund that can be redeemed for USDC under the offer.

Open the trustline through the verified QR page

For Stellar assets except XLM, an account must explicitly add a trustline. For USDM, use eurmtl.me/asset/USDM: it shows the same issuer as the official stellar.toml and StellarExpert.

Open trustline with QR

Open the QR page

Go to eurmtl.me/asset/USDM. The page says “Opening trustline for token USDM” and shows issuer GDHDC4…UUSDM.

Verify the issuer

Before signing in your wallet, make sure asset code = USDM and issuer = GDHDC4GBNPMENZAOBB4NCQ25TGZPDRK6ZGWUGSI22TVFATOLRPSUUSDM.

Choose a wallet

The page provides “Add to Sunce / Lobstr” and “Add to MMWB” options. You can also scan the QR if your wallet supports Stellar URI.

Sign changeTrust

Your wallet creates a changeTrust operation. Never enter a seed phrase on a website; signing should happen only inside your wallet.

Receive USDM

After confirmation, the account can receive USDM, display balances and trade the token through Stellar DEX.

How to get, trade and redeem USDM

Direct routes: buy through the USDM/USDC market, receive by transfer, open a trustline, and redeem under the public offer through the MTL Fund.

Get / buy

  1. Open a USDM trustline through the verified QR page.
  2. Fund your Stellar wallet with USDC or XLM.
  3. Use StellarX or another Stellar DEX interface for the USDM/USDC pair.
  4. USDM can also be received by transfer from another Stellar account or through Montelibero ecosystem operations.
StellarX USDM/USDCOpen trustline with QR

Redeem to USDC

  1. Legal basis: “USDM Token Public Offer” on Montelibero.org.
  2. Stated rule: USDM → USDC on Stellar one-for-one within 24 hours after the holder’s application is received.
  3. Operational MTL Fund questions: official support bot @mtl_helper_bot.
  4. After the request and USDM transfer, the holder expects an equal USDC transfer back to the Stellar account specified/used in the request.

Risks and technical facts

Keep this section on the site: it builds trust and reduces the chance that users confuse USDM with another asset.

Issuer risk

The 1:1 redemption is based on an MTL Fund obligation. USDM is not Circle USDC and not a bank deposit.

Liquidity and DEX price

Stellar DEX can show a price above or below 1 USDC due to orders and liquidity. Redemption under the offer is a separate mechanism.

Ticker collisions

USDM on CoinMarketCap/CoinGecko usually refers to other projects. For USD Montelibero, the key checks are Stellar, issuer GDHDC4…UUSDM and domain mtl.montelibero.org.

Issuer unlocked / unlimited

StellarExpert shows issuer account lock status: unlocked, and stellar.toml contains is_unlimited=true. So a hard cap is not the trust mechanism; issuer rules and public reporting matter.

Self-custody

Stellar transactions are irreversible. Lost seed phrases or transfers to wrong addresses are usually not recoverable.

Regulation

Tokens and stablecoins can be regulated differently across jurisdictions. For commercial use, assess your own legal and tax duties.

FAQ

Short answers for a user seeing USDM for the first time.

What is USDM?

USDM / USD Montelibero is a dollar stablecoin on Stellar issued by the MTL Fund. Official sources state that the issuer undertakes to redeem it for USDC one-for-one on Stellar within 24 hours after a holder request.

Where is the official offer?

Direct link: https://montelibero.org/2023/05/12/usdm_token_public_offer/. On the site it is titled “Токен USDM. Публичная оферта”.

Where is the official GitHub?

MTL Fund repository: https://github.com/montelibero-org/mtl. Asset metadata is published in .well-known/stellar.toml.

How do I open a trustline?

Click “Open trustline with QR” or go to https://eurmtl.me/asset/USDM. Verify the issuer and sign changeTrust in your wallet.

Why does Stellar require a trustline?

On Stellar, every issued asset except XLM requires an explicit trustline to a specific issuer. This prevents users from accidentally receiving arbitrary assets.

Is USDM the same as USDC?

No. USDC is a separate token from a different issuer. USDM is an MTL Fund token that can be redeemed for USDC under the offer.

Can I buy USDM with USDC?

Yes, through Stellar DEX, for example with the direct StellarX USDM/USDC link. The market rate depends on the order book.

How do I avoid a fake?

Check all three fields together: code = USDM, issuer = GDHDC4GBNPMENZAOBB4NCQ25TGZPDRK6ZGWUGSI22TVFATOLRPSUUSDM, domain = mtl.montelibero.org.

Does this site ask for a seed phrase?

No, and it should not. Trustline and payment signing happen in a wallet. Any site asking for your seed phrase is dangerous.

Where do I ask MTL Fund questions?

The MTL Fund README lists the support bot: @mtl_helper_bot. For the legal basis of USDM, use the USDM offer.
